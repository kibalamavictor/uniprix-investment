About us

Uniprix Investment is a Construction and Project management company specializing in project Planning, Construction, Interior designs, and Renovation and Property management services.

We believe every successful Construction project starts with proper planning from Site Visits, Project designs to construction, focusing on Accuracy, Quality control, Efficient and Execution at every stage.
