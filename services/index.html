Services

We offer a range of services that help clients plan better, manage efficiently, and build with confidence.

At Uniprix Investment, we offer end-to-end solutions that support clients from the earliest planning stages through construction, interior design, and project completion. Our services are designed to ensure clarity, quality, and confidence at every stage.

Architectural & Structural Design

We create detailed architectural plans and structural designs tailored to your vision, site conditions, and budget — ensuring safety, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.

Includes
Concept & schematic drawings
Full working drawings
Structural engineering plans
Permit-ready documentation

Construction & Project Management

From groundbreaking to handover, we manage every aspect of the build — coordinating contractors, materials, timelines, and quality control so your project is delivered on time and on budget.

Includes
Site supervision & coordination
Contractor procurement
Progress reporting
Quality assurance inspections

Interior Design & Finishing

Our interior design team transforms spaces into functional, beautiful environments — selecting materials, finishes, furniture, and lighting that reflect your style and purpose.

Includes
Space planning & mood boards
Material & finish selection
Furniture & décor sourcing
Full fit-out supervision

Renovation & Remodelling

We breathe new life into existing structures — from residential upgrades to full commercial overhauls — with minimal disruption and maximum impact.

Includes
Site assessment & feasibility
Demolition & structural changes
MEP upgrades (electrical, plumbing)
Final finishing & handover

Real Estate Consultation

We guide investors and homeowners through Uganda's real estate landscape — from site identification and valuation to market analysis and investment strategy.

Includes
Land & property search
Market & investment analysis
Due diligence & title verification
Negotiation support
